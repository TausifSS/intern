AI Tools You Should Know

Sports AI Tools

  • Catapult Sports: Wearable technology that tracks athlete performance metrics in real time.
  • StatSports: GPS-based player tracking systems used by professional sports teams.
  • AI Video Analytics: Analyzes match footage to provide insights into tactics, formations, and player movement.

Study AI Tools

  • Grammarly: An AI-powered writing assistant that helps with grammar, tone, and clarity in essays and papers.
  • Duolingo: A language learning app that adapts to users' learning pace using AI.
  • Socratic: An AI-powered tutor that helps students solve math, science, and history problems.

Business AI Tools

  • HubSpot: AI-driven marketing and customer relationship management (CRM) platform.
  • Zoho Analytics: AI-based business intelligence platform that provides data-driven insights.
  • ChatGPT for Customer Service: AI-powered chatbots that assist in real-time customer support.

Creative Arts AI Tools

  • RunwayML: AI-powered platform for creating unique images, videos, and animations.
  • AIVA: An AI composer that creates music based on your input.
  • Deep Dream Generator: Transforms images into dream-like creations using AI algorithms.
